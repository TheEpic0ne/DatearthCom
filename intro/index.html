Datearth

Datearth is a geopolitical Minecraft server with a real world earth map where towns and nations strive for power with diplomacy and war. You can play by connecting to play.datearth.com with version 1.13.2 to the latest version.

Worlds

There are two worlds on Datearth. An ancient world with a vanilla experience where you fight with swords and bows and ride horses. A modern world with a feature-packed experience where there are guns, vehicles and nuclear technology. Download the resource pack if you are playing on the modern world at datearth.com/resourcepack to view guns and vehicles.
