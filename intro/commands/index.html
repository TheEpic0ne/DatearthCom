General

  • /vote - Vote to receive a mission.
  • /balance - Check your money balance.
  • /baltop - View the top balances.
  • /top - Open the leaderboards menu.
  • /trade - Request to trade with a player.
  • /weapons - Opens the weapon shop.
  • /vehicles - Opens the vehicle shop.
  • /garage - Opens your vehicle garage.
  • /gold - Open the gold shop.
  • /rocket - Purchase a space rocket.
  • /moon - Travel to the moon with your space rocket.

Chat

  • /channel - View channel commands.
  • /join [channel] - Join a chat channel.
  • /leave [channel] - Leave a chat channel
  • /g [message] - Join or send a message in global channel.
  • /l [message] - Join or send a message in local channel.
  • /tc [message] - Join or send a message in town channel.
  • /nc [message] - Join or send a message in nation channel.
  • /tr [message] - Join or send a message in trade channel.
  • /msg - Send a private message to a player.
  • /ignore - Block messages from a player.

Town

  • /t ? - Shows town commands available.
  • /t new - Creates a new town.
  • /t spawn - Teleports you to your town’s spawn.
  • /t claim - Mayor command to claim the townblock in which you stand for your town.
  • /t unclaim - Mayor command to unclaim the townblock in which you stand.
  • /t add - Mayor command to add residents to your town.
  • /t kick - Mayor command to remove residents from your town.
  • /t online - Shows players in your town which are online.
  • /t withdraw - Removes money from town bank.
  • /t deposit - Adds money from player to the town bank.
  • /t set - Set board, mayor, homeblock and more.
  • /t toggle - Toggle pvp, tax and more.
  • /t ranklist - Displays residents and their ranks.
  • /t rank - Grants or removes a rank to a resident of the town.
  • /t say - Broadcast a message to online town members.
  • /t delete - Mayor command to delete a town.
  • /t leave - Leaves a town.
  • /t join - Join a town that doesn’t require invites.
  • /t here - Shows you the town screen of the town in which you stand.

Nation

  • /n ? - Shows nation commands.
  • /n new - Mayor command to create a nation.
  • /n add - Invites a town to your nation.
  • /n kick - Removes a town from your nation.
  • /n ally - Add or remove a nation to your nation’s ally list.
  • /n enemy - Add or remove a nation to your nation’s enemy list.
  • /n rank - Grants or removes a rank to a resident of the nation.
  • /n say - Broadcast a message to online nation members.
  • /n set - Set king, capital and more.
  • /n withdraw - King command to remove money from the nation bank.
  • /n deposit - King command to add money to the nation bank.
  • /n online - Shows players in your nation which are online.
  • /n leave - Mayor command to leave the nation they are a part of.
  • /n delete - Deletes your nation.
  • /n list - Lists nations.
