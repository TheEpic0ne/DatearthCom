Earth

Here you can find all overworld resources. Crates with supplies drop regularly at random places on earth. You can right-click a compass for directions to the nearest crate from you.

Moon

Here you can find all end resources. In the modern world, aliens inhabit this planet and have a low chance of dropping end rods or their heads which carries special powers. In the ancient world, the moon dragon inhabits this planet which drops itself as a rideable mount. The moon dragon respawns every 5 days.

Mars

In the modern world, the planet Mars is present instead of the nether. Here you can find all the nether resources. Mars can be accessed through nether portals. Martians inhabit this planet and have a low chance of dropping a nether star or their head which carries special powers.
